Who We Are

Mission

To provide high-quality consulting, training, and certification services, tailored to the specific needs of individuals and organizations, contributing to their professional development and success in a constantly changing world.

Vision

To become a recognized leader in training and consulting, setting standards of excellence and creating opportunities for sustainable personal and professional growth for our customers across Canada and beyond.

Values

  • Excellence: Committed to providing the highest quality services.
  • Innovation: Integrating modern, creative solutions to meet changing needs.
  • Inclusion: Promoting equity, diversity, and accessibility in all our services.
  • Collaboration: Working in partnership with our customers and stakeholders to achieve meaningful results.
  • Ethics: Operating with integrity, transparency, and respect for professional standards.

Objectives

  • Offer training and certification that is recognized and relevant to the job market.
  • Help companies improve their performance through customized consulting services.
  • Create a network of local and international partners to enrich learning opportunities.
  • Set up programs to promote the professional integration of minorities and under-represented groups.
  • Establish Vancouver Consulting Academy as a trusted name in education and professional development.

Our Team

Our team comprises experienced professionals dedicated to providing innovative and customized solutions for our clients' success.
